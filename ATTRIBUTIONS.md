Previously used app for this purpose:
-------------------------------------
    1.  GPS Test by Chartcross Limited
        ([com.chartcross.gpstest](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.chartcross.gpstest)) 
        the app I used for speed readout. Replicated gps speedometer feature and HUD mode.  
        

    2.  “Mileage Tracker by Driversnote” by “Driversnote Mileage Tracker”
        ([com.driversnote.driversnote](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.driversnote.driversnote))
        the app I used to keep track of distance travelled. Replicated travel
        log idea, and implemented my own odometer to know when to service
        vehicle.
